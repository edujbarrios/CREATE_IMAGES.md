# Radiology Extension

The radiology extension governs synthetic, non-diagnostic medical imaging workflows across X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, PET, OCT, dermoscopy, endoscopy, and histopathology.

Radiology extensions must preserve:

- Synthetic-only generation.
- Non-diagnostic labeling.
- Anatomy-aware validation.
- Modality-aware prompting.
- Acquisition-aware constraints.
- No real patient data.
- No fake reports or fake DICOM exports.
